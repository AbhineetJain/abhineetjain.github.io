About me

I am a final year undergraduate student, pursuing Computer Science and Engineering at IIIT Hyderabad. I am involved in undergraduate research at Robotics Research Centre, under the guidance of Dr. K. Madhava Krishna.

My areas of interest are Humanoid robots, Convolutional Neural Networks, and Computer Vision. I spent two months in Summer 2016 at Robotics Embedded Systems Laboratory in University of Southern California, under Dr. Gaurav S. Sukhatme.

I also conduct weekly tutorials and curate assignments, as a Teaching Assistant at IIIT Hyderabad, for core freshman courses like Computer Programming. In previous semesters, I have been a TA for Digital Logic and Processors, and Computer Systems Organization courses as well.

I have been elected as a Member of Students' Parliament, for two years in a row now. This year, I serve as the Speaker of the Parliament. I am also an active participant in cultural activities on campus including dance performances, and plays.

Apart from academics, watching movies, and TV shows serves as great source of relaxation. I also write poems and like to dance, when I am overwhelmed, to pour my heart out.
